A Story About Anthony Edwards.

Anthony Edwards is an American Basketball player. He plays for the Nba Team "Minnesota Timberwolves" and is a shooting guard. He was born on Aug 5, 2001 (22 yrs old). He went to Holy Spirit Preparatory School in his hometown of Atlanta, Georgia. He was a Five Star recruit in highschool. In the NBA currently he averges 25.9 Pts, 35.1 Mins, 46.1 FG%, 35.7 3pt%, 5.4 Reb, and 5.1 Ast in the 2023-2024 Season.
